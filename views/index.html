Our Next Meetup

The next meeting of LNUG is on the 24th October 2012.

Doors will open at 6:30pm where we will have Beer and Pizza (!) , with our talks starting at 7pm.

  • Integrating Node.js into your existing technology stack - Andrew Nesbitt
  • The future of wearable computing with node.js, Raspberry PI and bowler hats - Paul Serby
  • Writing real-time games in coffeescript with node.js + socket.io & HTML5 - Peter Johnson and Tancredi Trugenberge

Bring along your laptops and work on node projects, pair with others on cool hacks or open source projects or just hang out and chat with all the usual refreshments.
